ActvMile

A simple Bookmarklet that allows you to quickly post a workout from MOTOACTV to dailymile.

Install Bookmarklet

To use this tool, drag the button on the right to your Bookmarks bar:

ActvMile

LOCAL

Use It

Then navigate to a workout on MOTOACTV.com, fill in your journal and then click the new ActvMile button in your toolbar. This will create a new dailymile workout entry and redirect you to the entry.
